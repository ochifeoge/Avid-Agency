We measure the success of a campaign using key performance indicators (KPIs) such as:
  1. Engagement Rate: Likes, comments, and shares on your posts.
  2. Reach and Impressions: How many people see your content.
  3. Click-Through Rate (CTR): The percentage of people who click on your ads or posts.
  4. Conversion Rate: The number of users who take a desired action, such as making a purchase or signing up for a newsletter.
  5. Return on Investment (ROI): The revenue generated compared to the cost of the campaign.
